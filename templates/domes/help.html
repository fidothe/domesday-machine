Understanding the map

What information is recorded under 'Statistics'?

  • The name is the modern equivalent of the Domesday manor/vill name. (If you can help add the original Domesday names, please get in touch.)
  • The area (which often doesn't exist) is the name of area intermediate between county and hundred/wapentake, such as lathes, rapes, or ridings.
  • The hundred is the Domesday hundred in which the manor is described, or to which it belongs. A hundred (also known as a wapentake) is a Saxon division of land, smaller than a county.
  • The county is the Domesday county. This may be different from modern counties. In particular, Lancashire doesn't exist in Domesday Book.
